Meat Recipe

Description

This recipe is about how to make a delicious beef stew which will make you lick your hands for more. It doesn't require any expensive or explicit ingridients.

Ingridients

  • I hate to say this but you do require meat
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Water
  • Chilli
  • Coriander
  • Carrot

Steps

  1. Boil the meat to to a tender taste.
  2. Pour oil on a pan and add onions while gently heating the pan
  3. Add the tender meat in the pan and start mixing
  4. Add tomatoes and chilli and wait to form a thick paste
  5. Add cut carrot and coriander
  6. Add little water to get soup
  7. Wait a few minutes before serving
